He lectures at Fontys University Venlo and does PhD research at Fontys Venlo, TU/e Eindhoven, and ITU Copenhagen, focusing on deep learning in medical diagnostics. He earned his Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Master’s degree in Data Science from Maastricht University.
